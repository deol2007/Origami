Interesting Facts about Camel

Camel

  1. Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  2. Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.
  3. There are two types of camels: One humped or “dromedary” camels and two humped Bactrian camels.

Interesting Facts about pigeon

  • Pigeons are one of only a small number of species to pass the 'mirror test' a test of self recognition.
  • Pigeons can also recognise each letter of the human alphabet
  • The rarest pigeon is the pink pigeon.

Interesting Facts about panda

Panda

  • Giant pandas have six fingers.
  • Pandas have 42 teeth most of which are flat crushing molars
  • Giant pandas can grow 2-3 feet tall.
